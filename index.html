Global Suicide Rate per 100k population

Firstly, when examining the global suicide rate, it is evident that Russia exhibits the highest prevalence of suicides 11305 per 100,00 population. The second highest country with the most suicide rate was Korea 9350 per 100,000 population.

Female Vs Male

Upon compsrison of the overall suicide committed between male and female, it can be found that men are more likely to commit suicide as compared to women.

Stacked Bar Chart for the Top 10 Countries with Highest Suicide Rate per Age Group

Lithuania has the highest suicide rate per its population where the majority comprises of 55-74 age group & 35-54 age group with suicide rates of 2496 and 2696. Korea has the hgihest suicide rate for 75+ age group with a suicide rate of 4294 per its population.

Average Suicide Rates per 100k population across Age Groups

There is an increase in suicide rate for all age group between year 1994-1996 except for 5-14 years age group. The elderly age group of 75+ years has the highest suicide rate reaching 4709/100K population in 1995. On the contrary, younger age group such as those from 5-14 years old are least concerning, exhibiting a constant trend throughout time.
